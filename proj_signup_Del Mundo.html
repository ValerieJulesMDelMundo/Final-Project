Partnership Campaign

Bukas Palad: Center for Life Program

We partnered with Bukas Palad, a non-profit and non-governmental organization that provides sustainable development, education, livelihood, information and communication strategy to children, elderly, urban poor, and victims of calamities and disasters. We specifically partnered with their Center of Life Program which helps guide new parents through their first steps of parenthood. The organization is currently looking for volunteers. If you believe you have the capabilities to assist this campaign and their cause, please fill out the sign up form at the side of this page.
