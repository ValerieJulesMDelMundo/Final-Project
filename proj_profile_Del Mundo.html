Bukas Palad: Center for Life Program

About Bukas Palad

The "Bukas Palad Program," or Bukas Palad Foundation of the Philippines, is a non-profit organization that provides sustainable development, education, livelihood, and community support to children, the elderly, urban poor, and disaster victims. The organization aims to empower individuals and communities through holistic development, focusing on education, health, nutrition, and community development.

About the Program

The "Center for Life Program" offered by the Bukas Palad Foundation of the Philippines, Inc. (BP) focuses on both the protection of life and the promotion of responsible parenthood. This program aims to strengthen the rapport between spouses, potentially through family formation and counseling services, and supports the overall well-being of families and individuals.
